# 01 — Configure Microsoft as Identity Provider

## Outcome
Tailscale authenticates users through Microsoft. Only your Microsoft
account can join this tailnet.

## Steps

### 1. Go to Tailscale and Get started
Presented with native built in identity provider, click your prefence. 

![alt text](<../Resources/Screenshot 2026-05-22 10.45.09 PM.png>)

### 2. Authorise with Microsoft
Click Connect and sign in with your personal Microsoft account.
Tailscale handles the OAuth app registration itself — no Azure portal
setup required.

### 3. Confirm the connection
Under Identity provider you should your selected identitiy provider of choice in this case Microsoft

![alt text](<../Resources/Screenshot 2026-05-22 10.45.37 PM.png>)
